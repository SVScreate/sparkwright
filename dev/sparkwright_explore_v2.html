Sparkler tip variations

Three takes on a single bright source with radiating lines — the tip of a lit sparkler. The difference between them is density, length, and how "wild" the energy feels. All share the same amber-to-gold color and glow.

Variant S1

Tight Spark

Fewer, longer lines. More restrained — closer to a refined starburst. Reads cleanest at very small sizes.
